This edited volume presents a broad view of sonic interaction in immersive media,
from perception and spatial hearing to interaction design, rendering methods,
and practical applications in virtual and mixed reality.

It collects contributions from international researchers and practitioners,
organizing theory, methods, and case studies in a single reference for the field.
